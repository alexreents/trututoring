What is peer-to-peer learning?

Our approach toward tutoring involves student-to-student interaction. We utilize the expertise of high schoolers who've already demonstrated academic success in the specific subject of need. These tutors are familiar with both the local curriculum and the nature of student learning. They are, after all, students themselves.

Peer-to-peer interaction has many benefits both in the classroom and outside. Click below to find out more about our method of learning!
