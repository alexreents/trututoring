Welcome to TRU Tutoring

TRU Tutoring is a peer-to-peer education startup, dedicated to providing quality learning services at an affordable price.

Our service connects older and younger students to foster a more engaging and meaningful learning experience at a fraction of the cost.

Join Us!

To learn more, get in contact with us, submit a tutor application, or view testimonials, visit our facebook page!

Students & Parents

Recieve one-on-one tutoring in any subject at hourly prices ranging from $0-$20. Our tutors are distinguished both in their subject-specific knowledge and overall instruction quality!

Tutors

With TRU, high school and college-age students can gain valuable professional experience tutoring younger students. Our dynamic model allows for flexibility both in hours and compensation. Applications are currently open!

COVID-19

In these uncertain times, affordable education has never been more important. TRU is committed to continue providing quality peer-to-peer learning opportunities, now by using remote videoconferencing technology.
