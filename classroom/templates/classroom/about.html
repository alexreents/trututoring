About TRU Tutoring

Mission: Provide peer-to-peer tutoring services that make quality learning truly affordable.

Core Beliefs: TRU Tutoring believes that learning from a peer who has a shared academic experience is the most effective supplement to learning in the classroom. Peer to peer tutoring interactions facilitate positive environments for two way learning.

We believe that “to teach is to learn twice.”

We also believe that learning done in the classroom is only a portion of a students learning experience. And yet, tutoring services are far too expensive for many families. People should have access to affordable education without sacrificing quality.

Peer-to-peer: Peer to peer tutoring increases retention rates and comprehension of material, empowers students to learn and teach, and creates an environment of “inclusive excellence.”
