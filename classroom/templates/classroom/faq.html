Frequently Asked Questions

"Peer-to-peer" is a style of learning around which we've founded our business. The central idea is that students will teach others students, making it easier be confused, ask questions, and actuall begin learning. To learn more about the philosophy behind our style of tutoring, visit our 'About' page!
To become a tutor, visit our signup page. You can get started tutoring right away, but to become TRU verified you will submit an application to further demonstrate your qualifications.
Verification is a system that we use distinguish tutors who have been interviewed, vetted, and determined to have expertise in the subject they teach. For students, we highly recommend choosing a tutor that has been TRU Verified.

For tutors, recieving verification is a relatively straightforward process. You will submit a resume and basic application, and will complete an interview to assess subject-specific knowledge and general tutoring capabilities. Being TRU Verified makes it more likely for students to request a lesson with you. With verification, you recieve additonal support from TRU Tutoring and have access to bonus incentives. To begin this process, please email info@trututoring.live!
Ensuring safety of both our students and tutors is a top priority for TRU Tutoring. Regardless of verification, we highly recommend reaching out to your tutor/student before the first lesson to get familiarity with who you'll be working.

Unfortunately, it is impossible for us to verify the identiy of every tutor or student that uses our platform. We are a matching service and our tutors are independent contrators, so students and tutors each have a responsibility to establish a safe connection.
We offer subjects in nearly any area of K-12 instruction! Subjects are limited only by the amount of tutors avaialable for that subject. Most tutors are for Math and Science, but any topic is possible! We also offer support in SAT/ACT test prep, as well as college application preparation, essay writing, and general advising (tutors are current college studetns).

You can find a complete list of subjects on the 'Sign Up' page.
Lessons with TRU Verified tutors cost a flat rate of $22.50, with the first lesson free. Independent tutors have the ability to set their own price. To learn more about pricing, please visit our 'Prices' page.
